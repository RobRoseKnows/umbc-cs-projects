Head/Tail Quality Comments¶

From just the head() and tail() commands, it looks like the data is pretty good for several of the columns (Citation, Tag, ExpMM, ExpYY, State, Make, Address, ViolCode, Description, ViolFine, Balance, OpenFine, OpenPenalty, ImportDate and Location) but is missing pretty much universally for several of the other columns (Neighborhood, PoliceDistrict, CouncilDistrict). It looks like some of the columns are only conditionally filled like PenaltyDate and NoticeDate).

Column Content¶

I chose to work with the columns that were totally filled in. Those Columns ended up being State, ViolCode, ViolFine, OpenFine, OpenPenalty and Balance. Although ImportDate and Description were also not missing any values, I chose not do anything with them because it looked like Description was just the prose description of the violation type, and therefore not very interesting to play with and ImportDate didn't seem to mean much about the data, more so when the data was entered.
